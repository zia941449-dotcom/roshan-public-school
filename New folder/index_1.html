FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What documents are required for admission? +

Required documents include: Birth Certificate, Previous school report card, CNIC or B-Form copy, 4 passport-size photographs, Transfer certificate from previous school (for Grades 2 and above), and Medical fitness certificate.

What is the fee structure? +

Fees vary by grade level. One-time admission fee ranges from PKR 15,000 to 25,000. Monthly tuition ranges from PKR 8,000 to 18,000 depending on grade. Annual charges include lab fees, library fees, and examination fees. Contact our accounts office for a detailed breakdown.

Is there an entrance test? +

Yes, an entrance assessment is required for students applying to Grades 6 and above. It covers English, Mathematics, and General Knowledge. For Pre-Primary to Grade 5, a simple oral assessment is conducted. The test is designed to understand the child's current level, not to reject applicants.

Is there a scholarship programme? +

Yes! We offer merit-based scholarships covering 25% to 100% of tuition fees for academically outstanding students. Need-based financial assistance is also available. Please contact the principal's office with relevant documentation to apply for financial aid.

What are the school timings? +

School hours are Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 2:30 PM. Pre-Primary students have shorter hours: 8:00 AM to 12:30 PM. After-school activities run from 2:30 PM to 4:30 PM. The school is closed on Saturdays, Sundays, and all public holidays.

Is transport available? +

Yes, the school provides a safe and comfortable transport service covering major areas of the city. GPS-tracked buses with trained drivers and female attendants (for girls' buses) are available. Transport fees are charged separately. Contact our transport office for route details.

What is the medium of instruction? +

The primary medium of instruction is English. Urdu and Islamic Studies are taught in Urdu. We follow a Cambridge-aligned curriculum for Grades 9 and above, and a national curriculum with enhancements for lower grades.

Can I visit the school before applying? +

Absolutely! We welcome school visits every Tuesday and Thursday from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM. Please call ahead or use the contact form to schedule your visit. Guided tours are conducted by our admissions team and include classroom observations and facility tours.
